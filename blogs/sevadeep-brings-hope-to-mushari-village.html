Inauguration of Educational Center by sevadeep Foundation Brings New Hope to Mushari Village

Mushari Village, Khagaria District: In a remarkable initiative to address the lack of educational facilities in Mushari village, sevadeep Foundation, under the guidance of its founder, Shri Dunbahadur Das, inaugurated a Free Education Center. The inauguration ceremony took place in Mushari Ward No. 07 and witnessed the enthusiastic participation of the entire Musahar community residing in the region.

Mushari village, located in village Ward No. 7 of the Gogri police station area, had been grappling with limited access to formal education for the local children. Recognizing this critical need, sevadeep Foundation took the initiative to establish the education center, aimed at providing quality coaching and educational opportunities for the community's children.

The center's opening brought renewed hope and enthusiasm toward education among the Musahar community. On this auspicious occasion, Manjit Kumar, a passionate and dedicated youth from society, was appointed as the teacher, ensuring the children receive the best guidance and mentorship on their educational journey.

The event witnessed a heartwarming gathering of men and women from the Musahar community, all expressing their heartfelt gratitude to the foundation for its noble work. They emphasized that organizations like sevadeep Foundation play a pivotal role in uplifting the marginalized sections of society and providing them with opportunities to progress and succeed in life.

Speaking at the inauguration, Shri Dunbahadur Das stressed the importance of education in empowering communities and bringing about positive change. He highlighted the foundation's commitment to supporting the welfare of those often overlooked and underserved, ensuring they have access to a brighter future.

With the new Free Education Center now operational in Mushari village, the children of the Musahar community can look forward to a better and brighter tomorrow. The sevadeep Foundation's initiative serves as a beacon of hope, inspiring other organizations and individuals to contribute to the upliftment of underserved communities.
