Empowering Women

At janseva R Foundation, we believe in promoting gender equality and empowering women to become self-sufficient contributors to their families and communities. As part of our commitment to this cause, we have launched an 'Empowering Women' initiative. Through this initiative, we aim to offer employment opportunities to women at janseva R Foundation's education centers and provide them with skill development opportunities.

Employment Opportunities

We actively encourage the employment of women at our coaching centers. Women can serve in various roles, including teachers, administrative staff, and support staff. By creating a diverse workforce, we not only promote gender equality but also provide children with positive female role models.

Skill Development

As part of our 'Empowering Women' initiative, we offer skill development programs to women from underprivileged backgrounds. These programs aim to enhance their employability and self-reliance. Some of the key components of our skill development initiatives include:

a. Sewing and Tailoring

We provide sewing machines and training to women, equipping them with valuable skills in sewing and tailoring. This enables them to start their small businesses or work as skilled tailors, generating income for themselves and their families.

b. Livestock Rearing:

We offer training and support to women interested in livestock rearing, such as goat farming. Livestock can be a sustainable source of income, and we teach the best practices for raising and managing these animals.

By incorporating the 'Empowering Women' initiative into our mission and vision, we aim to uplift not only children but also the women in the communities we serve. We believe that empowering women through education, employment, and skill development will have a positive ripple effect, creating a more inclusive and prosperous society for all.
