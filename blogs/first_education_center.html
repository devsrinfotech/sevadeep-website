Seva Deep Foundation Inaugurates First Educational Center, Fostering Hope for the Future

Gogri: On Wednesday, Seva Deep had opened their first ever free education centre as a measure to help underprivileged poor students receive the fruits of education in Shradda Nagar.

Retired teacher Shri Dunbahadur Das, a recipient of the prestigious National Award, inaugurated the education centre as the director of the institution. The event was attended by co-director Abhishek Kumar and esteemed guests Chandan Yadav, Prahlad Kumar, Gautam Kumar, and Raghunandan Das, among others.

Although unable to attend physically, Co Managing Director Shri Robin Smith made his presence felt through a video call, demonstrating his commitment to the cause. Nisha Kumari, an experienced educator, has been appointed as the head of the centre. The inauguration marked the beginning of a series of centres that will be established under the Seva Deep Foundation, all operating under the same society.

In a statement following the event, Director Shri Dunbahadur Das expressed his gratitude to everyone involved and emphasised the foundation's commitment to opening similar centres in the future. The aim is to extend educational opportunities to more deserving children, empowering them to build a better future for themselves and their communities.
