About Us

Our mission

1. Our educational NGO's mission is to provide access to quality education for all, regardless of their socio-economic background. We believe that education is the key to unlocking potential and creating positive change in society.

2. At Sevadeep, our mission is to provide quality primary education to underprivileged children, complementing the efforts of government schools. We believe that education is a powerful tool that can empower children to overcome the barriers of poverty and build a brighter future. Through our coaching centres, we aim to equip children with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for their holistic development.

Our Vision

1. Education is the key to empower next generation and fund the life of children with our love and support.

2. Our vision is to establish 500 plus coaching centres across the region, leveraging the latest technology and innovative teaching methods. We envision a future where every child, regardless of their socio-economic background, has access to quality education. By expanding our reach, we aim to create a strong network of centres that serve as beacons of hope, nurturing young minds and fostering a love for learning.

Where We Work

Sevadeep Foundation has taken significant strides in bridging the educational gap in remote villages by establishing five coaching centres. These centres have been set up in the following locations mainly situated in district Khagaria, Bihar with an strength of 40 students in each centre.
  • Sharada Nagar, Usri (Jamalpur, Gogri): Our 1st coaching centre was set-up in Sharada Nagar, Usri which aims to provide quality education and skill development opportunities to children who previously had limited access to such resources. There are nearly about 40 plus children getting education through our dedicated efforts, we are empowering these children to unlock their potential and pave their way towards a brighter future.
  • Chhoti Chak (Jamalpur, Gogri): In the village of Chhoti Chak, we have established our 2nd coaching centre to bring educational opportunities closer to the doorstep of children who were previously unable to access them. We believe that education is a catalyst for change, and through this centre, we strive to create a positive impact on the lives of these children.
  • Kurmi Tola (Jamalpur, Gogri): Sevadeep Foundation's 3rd coaching centre was inaugurated in Kurmi Tola aims to uplift the educational landscape of this remote village. By providing educational resources, qualified teachers, and a nurturing learning environment, we are fostering a love for learning and empowering children to break the barriers of limited opportunities.
  • Badi Chak (Jamalpur, Gogri): Located in Badi Chak, our 4th coaching centre serves as a beacon of hope for children who lacked access to quality education. Through our comprehensive educational programs, we are equipping these children with the necessary knowledge and skills to overcome challenges and create a brighter future for themselves.
  • Sishba (Parbatta): In the village of Sishba, we have established a our latest coaching centre to address the educational needs of children in this underserved community. By providing a conducive learning environment and educational resources, we are empowering these children to dream big and pursue their aspirations.

Our Works

We are committed to:

  • Providing Quality Education:


    We strive to ensure that each coaching centre maintains high educational standards, employing qualified teachers and implementing a well-rounded curriculum that nurtures intellectual, social, and emotional growth.

  • Leveraging Technology:


    Recognizing the importance of technology in education, we are dedicated to integrating innovative tools and resources into our coaching centres. By embracing digital learning platforms, we aim to enhance the learning experience and prepare children for the demands of the modern world.

  • Empowering Children:


    Our goal is not only to impart knowledge but also to empower children to become confident, compassionate, and responsible individuals. Through personalized attention, mentorship programs, and character-building activities, we in still values and life skills that will benefit them throughout their lives.

  • Collaboration and Community Engagement:


    We believe that collaboration with local communities, parents, and stakeholders is vital for the success of our mission. We actively seek partnerships, engage parents in their children's education, and work closely with the government and other organizations to create a comprehensive support system for the children we serve.

  • Measuring Impact:


    We are committed to monitoring and evaluating the impact of our coaching centres to ensure continuous improvement and accountability. Through regular assessments and data analysis, we strive to adapt our programs and strategies to meet the evolving needs of the children and communities we serve.
