Sevadeep Foundation

Sevadeep, as a non-profit organization, is dedicated to creating a positive impact on the lives of underprivileged children through education and healthcare initiatives. In addition to our core mission, we believe in empowering women by providing them with opportunities for employment and skill development.

Our Goals

Spread empathy through education

Increase donations equally across the country

Help more children and teens to get educated

Promote women empowerment through skill development and employment opportunities

Education Is The Key
To Empower Next Generation

Our educational NGO's mission is to provide access to quality education for all, regardless of their socio-economic background. Education is the key to unlocking potential and creating positive change in society.
At Sevadeep, our mission is to provide quality primary education to underprivileged children, complementing the efforts of government schools. Education is a powerful tool that can empower children to overcome the barriers of poverty and build a brighter future. Our coaching centers equip children with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for holistic development.
It is a not-for-profit association that focuses on transforming the educational geography of India. Registered under the Trusts Act, the association creates classrooms for children in small townlets with the support of original communities and Volunteer preceptors from across the globe. With formalized assignment plans and tutoring aids for a harmonious literacy experience, the children from government seminaries admit quality education for a promising and satisfying future.
