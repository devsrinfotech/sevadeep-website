FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the FAQs page of SevaDeep Foundation! In this section, you'll find answers to frequently asked questions about our mission, CSR opportunities, our plan to open 500 free educational centers, and how you can play a pivotal role in empowering young minds and women.

The SevaDeep Foundation's mission is to provide quality education to every child, regardless of their socio-economic background, by utilizing technology and digital structures and collaborating with local communities and volunteer educators.
The Foundation is committed to empowering women by providing them opportunities to become teachers at SevaDeep Education Centers, fostering gender equality, and enabling women to play a vital role in shaping the future of young minds.

The foundation creates classrooms in small townlets in India, working with support from local communities and volunteer preceptors. As of now, the foundation established five free educational centers. These centres have been set up in the following locations mainly situated in district Khagaria, Bihar with a strength of 40 students in each centre.

Yes, The SevaDeep Foundation is a not-for-profit association registered under the Trusts Act.

The SevaDeep Foundation offers various CSR opportunities for businesses and organizations to contribute to its mission of providing quality education to underprivileged children. These opportunities may include sponsoring classrooms, funding educational initiatives, supporting technology-driven projects, or participating in volunteer programs

Participating in The SevaDeep Foundation's CSR initiatives allows businesses and organizations to make a significant difference in the lives of underprivileged children by promoting access to quality education. It also enhances their corporate social responsibility profile, fostering a positive image and reputation for contributing to a meaningful cause.

For more information about The SevaDeep Foundation's CSR opportunities and how to contribute to their mission of transforming India's educational geography, please check our SevaDeep CSR opportunities or contact us directly. We will provide all the necessary details and guidance for interested parties.
